Jeff, ditto to Brandon0, and back to the topic of the actual server you built: what RAID controller are you using? It appears you are using the onboard controller on the motherboard which seems risky since the general consensus, including discussions on ServerFault, are that onboard RAID is 'RAID in name only.' i.e. IT, not the drives tend to be the source of catastrophic data loss. Are you not concerned that you'll have a lot more to worry about than just the rather poor SSD reliability you've written about elsewhere?
